Tarta de atun

Click here to go back to recipe's

This is like the tarta that my hermano Pablo like to do.

  1. Make the filler with atun, onion, egg, and other tasty things.
  2. Make the tarta, use a lot of filler. It has to be juicy.
  3. Cook with the oven.
  4. Serve the tarta. Everyone will love it.
